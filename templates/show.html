📘 About This App

The TodoList App helps you manage your daily tasks efficiently. You can add tasks with a title and description, view them in a table format, update them, or delete them as needed.

  • ✅ Simple and user-friendly interface
  • 📝 Add, edit, and remove tasks easily
  • 💾 Uses SQLite for lightweight data storage
  • 📱 Fully responsive layout with Bootstrap

This app is ideal for students, developers, and anyone who wants to stay organized and productive with minimal effort.
